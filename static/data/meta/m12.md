## High school diploma or higher
Adults 25+ with at least a high school diploma

### Why is this important?
High school completion is a foundational measure of opportunity. A higher value is generally more favorable.

### About the Data
Calculated from the U.S. Census Bureau's American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates (2019ľ2024), at the Census-tract level, with margins of error. ACS 5-year vintages overlap year to year, so the series is a rolling estimate; compare only non-overlapping periods.

_**Source**: U.S. Census Bureau, American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates._
