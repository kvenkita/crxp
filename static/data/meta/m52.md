## Average household size
Average number of people per occupied household

### Why is this important?
Household size affects housing demand, school enrollment, and crowding. Higher and lower values are not inherently better or worse.

### About the Data
Calculated from the U.S. Census Bureau's American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates (2014ľ2024), at the Census-tract level, with margins of error. ACS 5-year vintages overlap year to year, so the series is a rolling estimate; compare only non-overlapping periods.

_**Source**: U.S. Census Bureau, American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates._
