## Management & professional occupations
Employed residents in management, business, science, and arts occupations

### Why is this important?
This Census major occupational group is the standard proxy for professional/managerial (“white-collar”) work used in labor and stratification research; it captures the professional-managerial class distinct from service, sales/office, and blue-collar work. Higher and lower values are not inherently better or worse.

### About the Data
Calculated from the U.S. Census Bureau's American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates (2014–2024), at the Census-tract level, with margins of error. ACS 5-year vintages overlap year to year, so the series is a rolling estimate; compare only non-overlapping periods.

_**Source**: U.S. Census Bureau, American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates._
