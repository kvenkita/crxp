## Fair or poor health (adults)
Share of adults reporting fair or poor general health

### Why is this important?
Self-rated health is a holistic summary that strongly predicts future illness, health-care use, and mortality. A higher value generally signals greater need.

### About the Data
Model-based small-area estimates from the CDC PLACES project (2019–2023), which uses BRFSS survey data and multilevel regression & poststratification to estimate adult prevalence for every Census tract. Each estimate has a confidence interval; values with high uncertainty are flagged.

_**Source**: CDC PLACES (model-based small-area estimates)._
