## High blood pressure (adults)
Share of adults ever told they have high blood pressure

### Why is this important?
Hypertension is a leading, manageable risk factor for heart disease and stroke, and a marker of chronic-disease burden in a community. A higher value generally signals greater need.

### About the Data
Model-based small-area estimates from the CDC PLACES project (2017–2023), which uses BRFSS survey data and multilevel regression & poststratification to estimate adult prevalence for every Census tract. Each estimate has a confidence interval; values with high uncertainty are flagged.

_**Source**: CDC PLACES (model-based small-area estimates)._
