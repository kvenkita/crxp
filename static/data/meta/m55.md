## Owner cost burden
Owner households paying 30%+ of income on housing costs

### Why is this important?
Owner cost burden measures affordability stress among homeowners (with and without a mortgage). A higher value generally signals greater need.

### About the Data
Calculated from the U.S. Census Bureau's American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates (2019–2024), at the Census-tract level, with margins of error. ACS 5-year vintages overlap year to year, so the series is a rolling estimate; compare only non-overlapping periods.

_**Source**: U.S. Census Bureau, American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates._
