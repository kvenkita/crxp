## Limited-English-speaking households
Share of households where no one 14+ speaks English "very well"

### Why is this important?
Limited-English-speaking households signal where language access (translated materials, interpreters) matters most for services and civic participation. Higher and lower values are not inherently better or worse.

### About the Data
Calculated from the U.S. Census Bureau's American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates (2016–2024), at the Census-tract level, with margins of error. ACS 5-year vintages overlap year to year, so the series is a rolling estimate; compare only non-overlapping periods.

_**Source**: U.S. Census Bureau, American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates._
