## Tree canopy
Average percent tree-canopy cover (continuous), from NLCD Tree Canopy Cover

### Why is this important?
Tree canopy cools neighborhoods, cleans air and water, and supports health and habitat; the continuous canopy measure captures trees beyond forest land alone. A higher value is generally more favorable.

### About the Data
Derived from the USGS National Land Cover Database (2015–2023), a 30-metre satellite land-cover product. Tract values are area-weighted zonal statistics (exact pixel fractions) of the classified raster. As a wall-to-wall classification it carries no sampling margin of error.

_**Source**: U.S. Geological Survey, National Land Cover Database (NLCD)._
