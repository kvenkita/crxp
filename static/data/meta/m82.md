## Total jobs (workplace)
Number of jobs located in the tract (workplace employment, LEHD LODES WAC)

### Why is this important?
Workplace jobs show where economic activity concentrates — offices, warehouses, and commercial sites — which residential population counts alone cannot capture. Higher and lower values are not inherently better or worse.

### About the Data
Calculated from the U.S. Census Bureau's American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates (2014–2022), at the Census-tract level, with margins of error. ACS 5-year vintages overlap year to year, so the series is a rolling estimate; compare only non-overlapping periods.

_**Source**: U.S. Census Bureau, American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates._
