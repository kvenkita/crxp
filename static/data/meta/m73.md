## Developed land
Share of land that is developed (open space through high intensity)

### Why is this important?
Developed land captures the urban/built footprint and how fast it is expanding. A higher value generally signals greater need.

### About the Data
Derived from the USGS National Land Cover Database (2001–2021), a 30-metre satellite land-cover product. Tract values are area-weighted zonal statistics (exact pixel fractions) of the classified raster. As a wall-to-wall classification it carries no sampling margin of error.

_**Source**: U.S. Geological Survey, National Land Cover Database (NLCD)._
