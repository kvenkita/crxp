## Poverty rate
Share of people with income below the federal poverty level

### Why is this important?
The poverty rate is a foundational measure of economic hardship and need for services, and it shapes eligibility for many programs. A higher value generally signals greater need.

### About the Data
Calculated from the U.S. Census Bureau's American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates (2019–2024), at the Census-tract level, with margins of error. ACS 5-year vintages overlap year to year, so the series is a rolling estimate; compare only non-overlapping periods.

_**Source**: U.S. Census Bureau, ACS 5-Year Estimates._
