## Median household income
Median annual household income

### Why is this important?
Median household income is a core measure of economic well-being and a strong correlate of health, education, and opportunity. A higher value is generally more favorable.

### About the Data
Calculated from the U.S. Census Bureau's American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates (2014–2024), at the Census-tract level, with margins of error. ACS 5-year vintages overlap year to year, so the series is a rolling estimate; compare only non-overlapping periods.

_**Source**: U.S. Census Bureau, American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates._
