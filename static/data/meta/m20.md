## Median Household Income
Median Household Income by Census tract.

### Why is this important?
Median household income is a core measure of economic well-being and a strong correlate of health, education, and opportunity. A higher value is generally more favorable.

### About the Data
This indicator is calculated from the U.S. Census Bureau's American Community Survey (ACS) 5-Year Estimates for 2018–2023, aggregated to 2020 Census tract boundaries. Values are in U.S. dollars (inflation-adjusted to the survey year). Because ACS figures are survey estimates, small tracts carry larger margins of error; interpret year-to-year changes with care.

_**Source**: U.S. Census Bureau, American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates._
