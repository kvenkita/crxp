## Upstream sources

CRXP compiles and derives its indicators from public data. The CC BY 4.0
license above covers CRXP's compiled and derived dataset, **not** the upstream
sources, which remain under their own terms:

- U.S. Census Bureau, American Community Survey (public domain)
- CDC PLACES (public domain)
- USGS National Land Cover Database, incl. Tree Canopy Cover (public domain)
- NOAA / NCEI EOG VIIRS Nighttime Lights (public domain)
- Neighborhood names © OpenStreetMap contributors, licensed **ODbL**
  (<https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright>). The neighborhood-name layer is a
  derived database under ODbL; attribute OpenStreetMap when you use it.
